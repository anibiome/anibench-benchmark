# AniBench v2 mathematical methods

Status: executable release candidate. Public biological rank permission remains
false until independent authority, source-complete comparisons, empirical
calibration, hostile review, and release-decision receipts are bound to exact bytes.
The software surface remains AniBench v2; the Level-1 authority is the substantive
role-aware v3 candidate, and target-v2 numeric semantics are quarantined.

## Six non-interchangeable families

AniBench reports intensive biological resolution, extensive reconstruction
capacity, longitudinal resolution, causal architecture, personalized/sequential
learning, and population transport. Uncertainty and reuse are cross-cutting receipt
layers. No hidden overall scalar is emitted.

## Joint participant-event information

For participant-event type \(e\), the registered observation/design operator is
\(A_e\), nuisance-aware covariance is \(R_e\), and retained effective support is
\(n_e\):

\[
\mathcal I_e=n_eA_e^\mathsf{T}R_e^{-1}A_e,
\qquad
\mathcal I_{\mathrm{trial}}=\sum_e\mathcal I_e.
\]

An event is an explicit participant-time-measurement-intervention hyperedge.
Pairwise linkage does not establish undeclared higher-order jointness. Physical
measurement ancestry and covariance prevent duplicate panels, technical replicates,
or correlated high-frequency rows from being counted as independent information.

For target parameters \(\theta\), nuisance parameters \(\eta\), and nuisance prior
precision \(\Lambda_\eta\), AniBench uses the Schur complement

\[
\mathcal I_{\theta\mid\eta}=
\mathcal I_{\theta\theta}-
\mathcal I_{\theta\eta}
(\Lambda_\eta+\mathcal I_{\eta\eta})^{-1}
\mathcal I_{\eta\theta}.
\]

This discounts only nuisance structure explicitly represented in a correctly
specified joint model. Omitted or misspecified batch, site, missingness, selection,
or measurement-error structure remains a sensitivity and falsification target; the
Schur complement cannot correct a nuisance process that was never bound.

Materially non-positive-semidefinite information and non-positive-definite
covariance fail closed.

## Absolute contraction and conditional family-reference attainment

For frozen positive-definite prior precision \(\Lambda_0\),

\[
G=\Lambda_0^{-1/2}\mathcal I_{\theta\mid\eta}\Lambda_0^{-1/2},
\qquad
L_{\mathrm{abs}}=\frac{1}{2\ln 10}\log\det(I+G).
\]

This is local posterior-volume contraction under the declared design model, not an
empirical model score.

If a future family-specific operating-characteristic authority freezes a reference,
attainment can be evaluated in frozen prior-whitened biological directions \(b_j\),
not by sorting the candidate's eigenvalues. Biological coordinate identity must come
from a canonical external basis authority: diagonalizing a reference matrix does not
select a unique biological basis when eigenvalues repeat. Define

\[
\Sigma=(I+G)^{-1},
\quad q_j=b_j^\mathsf{T}\Sigma b_j,
\quad \ell_j^{\mathrm{eff}}=q_j^{-1}-1.
\]

For reference requirements \(\ell_j^*\),

\[
Y_f=100\frac{\sum_j\min\{\log(1+\ell_j^{\mathrm{eff}}),
\log(1+\ell_j^*)\}}{\sum_j\log(1+\ell_j^*)},
\]

with uncapped overflow

\[
O_f=\frac{\sum_j\log(1+\ell_j^{\mathrm{eff}})}
{\sum_j\log(1+\ell_j^*)}.
\]

The cap is direction specific: surplus depth cannot substitute for missing breadth.
A coverage curve may report the reference weight reaching declared target fractions.
The role-aware Level-1 candidate supplies no current reference requirements,
attainment percentages, or completion claim. All six family targets remain typed
unknown until their source-bound operating-characteristic authorities resolve. Any
future family attainment and uncapped ratio must remain paired, cannot compensate
across coordinates or families, and can never be averaged into an overall scalar.

## Intensive, extensive, and longitudinal quantities

Intensive resolution uses one explicitly joint-observed bundle and excludes the
population multiplier. Incompatible bundles are not unioned. Extensive information
uses retained participant support and correlation-adjusted repetitions:

\[
n_e^{\mathrm{eff}}=N_er_e\frac{k_e}{1+(k_e-1)\rho_e}.
\]

Longitudinal resolution is assembled inside participant-set lineages. Primary
summaries are participant-weighted medians of distinct within-person offsets and
span; maxima and global calendar coverage remain audits.

## Causal, personalized, and transport quantities

Whole-policy and component allocation-support geometry are separate:

\[
\mathcal S_k=X_k^\mathsf{T}W_kX_k,
\qquad k\in\{\mathrm{policy},\mathrm{component}\}.
\]

Contrast columns are centered and range normalized so coding translations or scale
changes cannot inflate structural support. Component rank is derived from actual
assignment geometry, never arm count. These matrices omit outcome residual
covariance, effect size, and estimator performance and are not inferential precision
or biological information.

Sequential expected design information is

\[
\mathcal I_{\mathrm{seq}}=
\sum_{i,t}\pi_{it}(1-\pi_{it})B_{it}B_{it}^\mathsf{T},
\]

for eligible repeated decisions, known non-degenerate propensities, prospectively
measured pre-decision moderators, and linked later outcomes or biology.

Transport geometry requires distinct participant sets, common positive policy
support, linked outcomes, a defined target population, and source-bound common raw
context covariates with joint-support and positivity authority. Those covariates live
outside the 64-coordinate map; `T01-T08` are transport estimands, not context labels.
Site or country count alone earns no transport rank.

## Typed uncertainty and evidence

Coordinates are exact, interval, conditional, unknown, or absent. Unknown and absent
are not zero. Scenario envelopes recompile the complete protocol and bind their
scenario contract.

Design Preview, Registered Protocol, Realized, Accessible, and Demonstrated are
separate lanes. Identical protocol geometry receives identical capacity mathematics
in Design Preview and Registered Protocol; lane changes the claim authority, not the
numeric result.

## Level-1 role authority and source-bound comparison

The Level-1 builder consumes only the frozen biological coordinate registry, role
semantics, family questions, and explicit closure gates. Study or sponsor identities,
desired rankings, current leaders, and assay-menu counts are forbidden inputs.
Comparable public facts reference hash-pinned authority objects; they cannot inline
priors, operators, covariance, causal bases, or target geometry.

The 64-coordinate scientific map has seven disjoint roles:

- `S02-S08`, `S10-S16`, and `F01-F08`: 22 direct mutable outcomes;
- `S01`: baseline modifier;
- `S09`: exposure/context;
- `D01-D12`: longitudinal state-space estimands;
- `P01-P12`: causal-response estimands;
- `H01-H08`: heterogeneity operating characteristics; and
- `T01-T08`: transport estimands.

`D`, `P`, `H`, and `T` cannot be reused as direct outcomes, raw moderators, or raw
context strata. The six family authorities—intensive, extensive, longitudinal,
causal, personalized/sequential, and transport—are noncompensatory. Every current
family target and family enrollment remains typed unknown until its estimand,
operating-characteristic target, source-bound observation or identification objects,
and validation plan are executable.

There is no current overall scalar, target-completion percentage, stable rank, or
global enrollment. A global enrollment may be derived only after all family-specific
sample-size functions, joint context support, participant reuse, cross-family
covariance, aggregation, and rounding authorities resolve. Coordinate or modality
count cannot substitute for those objects. SMART and micro-randomized designs require
estimand-specific sample-size models; transport requires a defined target population,
joint support, conditional transportability, and positivity.

Primary design lineage:
[SMART dynamic-regime sample size, DOI 10.1177/0962280219877520](https://doi.org/10.1177/0962280219877520);
[micro-randomized proximal-effect sample size, DOI 10.1002/sim.6847](https://doi.org/10.1002/sim.6847);
[trial-to-target transportability, DOI 10.1093/aje/kwx164](https://doi.org/10.1093/aje/kwx164).

The previous target-v2 multiplicity, enrollment, context, and normalization semantics
are quarantined rather than inherited. The current candidate authority and executable
impact receipt are
`spec/v3/level1/role-aware-target-requirements.v3.json` and
`spec/v3/level1/migrations/v2-to-v3-substantive-impact-receipt.json`.

A source resolver verifies raw bytes, content hashes, exact locators, authority
references, and selected identifiers before constructing internal protocol geometry.
Missing geometry remains `not_scoreable`; it never becomes zero or an invented
matrix.

Future ranks require joint bootstrap or posterior draws under a frozen dependence
and multiplicity rule. Overlap of marginal intervals is not a ranking procedure; ties
require an exact numerical tolerance or preregistered practical-equivalence margin.

## Protocol-native optimization

The optimizer changes typed protocol geometry and recompiles every candidate. It
rejects direct score patches, weights, an overall objective, opposing duplicate
objectives, and resource changes without a positive source-bound delta. It returns a
Pareto frontier over declared family metrics and resource totals.
