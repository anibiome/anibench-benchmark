# How to read AniBench v2

AniBench asks: if this human experiment were the biological record available to a
future superintelligence, what could it reconstruct about state, change,
intervention response, individual variation, function, and population transfer?

## Read six families, never one overall score

1. **Intensive biological resolution** is the effective information in one
   explicitly joint participant-event bundle. More participants cannot buy depth.
2. **Extensive reconstruction capacity** is the retained, covariance-adjusted
   information accumulated across people and events.
3. **Longitudinal resolution** is within-person density, span, and retained
   trajectory support. Disjoint cross-sections do not create follow-up.
4. **Causal architecture** is the identifiable policy and operator-component
   geometry supported by randomized assignment and linked measured outcomes.
5. **Personalized and sequential learning** requires pre-decision state observers,
   repeated randomized decisions, linked readback, and an explicitly
   state-dependent decision rule. Moderator heterogeneity alone is not a
   personalized policy.
6. **Population transport** requires a defined source and target population, common
   outcome and policy estimands, source-bound joint context support, and positivity
   diagnostics. Site or country counts are not transport.

`overall_scalar` is always null. Compare a trial family by family or by Pareto
relations under declared resource constraints.

## Read the evidence lane separately

- **Design Preview**: capacity conditional on executing the declared protocol.
- **Registered Protocol**: the same geometry bound to a frozen registry or protocol.
- **Realized**: what was actually retained, linked, and quality controlled.
- **Accessible**: what an independent evaluator can lawfully and technically run.
- **Demonstrated**: held-out learning with calibration, nulls, and transfer receipts.

The lane changes the claim, not the design mathematics. Identical geometry receives
identical capacity in every lane. Missing later evidence never numerically punishes a
planned trial.

## Read unknown correctly

`exact`, `interval`, `conditional`, `unknown`, `absent`, and `not_scoreable` are
different states. Unknown geometry is never converted to zero, a midpoint, or an
invented observation matrix. A study can remain visible in the source atlas while
its family result is not scoreable.

## Why depth compounds

For a joint event, AniBench derives information from an observation operator and
covariance rather than assay names. Independent directions multiply the contraction
of the remaining posterior hypothesis volume; redundant directions saturate. The
reported log-volume contraction is therefore additive while the underlying remaining
volume changes multiplicatively.

## What Level 1 means now

Level 1 is a frozen scientific map plus an executable closure program, not a current
`100`, perfect-trial recipe, or participant target. The map preserves 64 coordinates
in six biological blocks and partitions them into seven disjoint estimation roles:
22 direct mutable outcomes; `S01` as a baseline modifier; `S09` as exposure/context;
and the 40 `D`, `P`, `H`, and `T` coordinates as longitudinal, causal,
heterogeneity, and transport estimands. Relational estimands cannot be counted again
as direct measurements, moderators, or context strata.

The six Level-1 family entries are noncompensatory and currently unresolved. Each
requires its own source-bound operating-characteristic authority. There is no overall
scalar, family target percentage, stable rank, or global enrollment value. Enrollment
becomes computable only after each family's estimand, error criterion, dependence and
missingness model, allocation process, validation design, joint context support, and
cross-family reuse/covariance authority are executable.

This does not penalize a planned trial. Design Preview compiles the declared
participant-event, trajectory, assignment, sequential-decision, and context geometry
exactly as proposed. It shows where a design gains information and which authority
objects remain open; it does not manufacture Level-1 completion while the reference
operating characteristics are unresolved.

The old target-v2 global multiplicity, enrollment, and completion outputs are
quarantined as archival provenance. The substantive impact receipt is
`spec/v3/level1/migrations/v2-to-v3-substantive-impact-receipt.json`; the current
candidate authority is
`spec/v3/level1/role-aware-target-requirements.v3.json`.

## Read a chart receipt

Every numerical chart ships with CSV or JSON, source-object SHA-256, exact locator,
derivation version, and a deterministic build receipt. Row order is not rank unless a
separate rank receipt explicitly says otherwise.
