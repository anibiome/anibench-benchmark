# Code of conduct

AniBench discussions must remain rigorous, specific, and respectful. Challenge formulas and evidence with sources, counterexamples, and reproducible tests. Harassment, intimidation, identity attacks, retaliation against challengers, and prestige-based dismissal are unacceptable.

Maintainers may remove abusive participation while preserving the scientific challenge record. Governance decisions disclose conflicts of interest and distinguish evidence disagreement from conduct enforcement.
