# AniBench agent entry point

Start with `CURRENT.md`, then follow its v2 authority order. Older v1 and alpha or
beta artifacts are archival pointers, not facts to inherit.

When changing AniBench:

- preserve the six non-interchangeable families: intensive, extensive,
  longitudinal, causal, personalized/sequential, and transport;
- keep evidence lane separate from capacity mathematics;
- keep exact, interval, conditional, unknown, and absent facts typed;
- derive information from participant-event geometry, observation operators,
  covariance, assignment, linkage, and source objects;
- reject additive modality menus, caller-provided ranks, patched family outputs,
  and hidden weights;
- never invent matrices or convert missing comparator geometry to zero;
- bind every displayed number to an exact source object, content hash, locator,
  and executable derivation;
- treat row order as non-rank unless a metric-specific rank receipt explicitly
  authorizes otherwise; and
- distinguish implemented, tested, packaged, remotely readable, biologically
  validated, and public-release states.

Design Preview is a real product lane. A planned protocol is evaluated on its
declared geometry without a numerical maturity penalty. Realized, Accessible, and
Demonstrated lanes require their corresponding evidence, but absence of that later
evidence does not reduce the planned design-capacity result.
