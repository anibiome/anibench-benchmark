# Roadmap

## v2.0 release candidate

- Freeze the six non-interchangeable capacity families and their typed input,
  result, authority, and uncertainty contracts.
- Publish the protocol-capacity compiler, protocol-native Pareto optimizer,
  source-bound external atlas, Trial Designer, OpenAPI surface, and exact replay
  receipts.
- Freeze a study-independent Level-1 authority derivation with executable
  sensitivity analysis and independent hostile review.
- Prove the fresh-history public repository, wheel, source distribution,
  installed runtime, browser workflow, licenses, metadata, and private-byte
  exclusion from one exact commit.
- Keep scalar ranking disabled; permit only source-complete family-specific and
  Pareto comparisons whose release receipt authorizes that claim.

## v2.x calibration and corpus expansion

- Complete independent source review for comparator protocol geometry without
  converting unresolved matrices to zero.
- Execute held-person, time, modality, intervention, and cohort task suites to
  test whether the capacity families predict reconstructive biological learning.
- Replace normative scenario envelopes with source-bound empirical covariance,
  retention, and observer-error estimates where valid.
- Add protocol, registry, and paper ingestion with field-level provenance and
  human adjudication before verified promotion.
- Add burden, cost, recruitment, ethics, and feasibility as separate design
  constraints; never hide them inside biological-capacity coordinates.
- Expand deterministic family charts and the interactive design frontier while
  preserving exact formulas, source hashes, and unknown states.

## Stable v2

- Independent methods and conflict-of-interest review bound to the exact release
  commit and authority bytes.
- Public challenge, correction, supersession, and Methods RFC processes.
- Signed immutable source, package, SBOM, checksum, and archival artifacts.
- Biological calibration claims only for task families that pass prespecified
  falsifiers and held-out validation.

## Later levels

- Increase spatial, temporal, molecular, tissue, environmental, functional, and
  interventional resolution without rewriting earlier Level-1 results.
- Add finer state, velocity, acceleration, observer-agreement, and
  intervention-operator task suites.
- Connect signed AniBench assessment packets to Anipedia and in-silico trial
  design systems while preserving evidence class and source provenance.
